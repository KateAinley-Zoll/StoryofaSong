On May 7th, 2021, Conan Gray released the official music video of Astronomy, a song that gained over 13 million views within 3 years. Produced by Tuff Contender, this song highlights a different part of love often overlooked by the usual chaos of a breakup or falling out: the pain of the inevitable fading of love. Gray uses thematic video choices, a strong central metaphor, and unique instruments to highlight themes of quiet heartbreak and distance, creating a turning point in romantic pop music and the struggles of relationships and how they are perceived in the digital age.

The official music video for Astronomy is filmed in a personalized way that emphasizes the personal connection between two people. The first-person perspective is a powerful artistic choice, making the audience feel as if they are experiencing the story themselves. The video begins with Gray and his love interest in a warm-colored room, evoking intimacy and closeness, like the warmth of love at its peak. Short clips of Gray and his partner climbing rocks and going on drives create a nostalgic tone. As the video progresses, the growing emotional distance is shown through subtle body language and lack of eye contact, mirroring the emotional drift highlighted in the lyrics. The scenes feel raw and authentic, matching the gentle delivery of the lyrics and adding to the emotional power of the piece.
